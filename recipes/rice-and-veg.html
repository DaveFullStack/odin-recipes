Rice & Veg Recipe

A simple, yet effective dish
Tastes like rice and vegetables

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Veg
  • A bit of pazazzzzz
  • The patience of a kung fu master

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil Water + plenty of salt
  2. Whack some oil in a pan of the frying sort and bang it on the heat like
  3. Chop Veg and start frying that shit up
  4. Add pazazzzzz to veg
  5. Add Rice when water has achieved boiling status
  6. Chill out for a second. Cut yourself some slack. It's tough times we're living in
  7. Once rice is ready whack it in with vegtables
  8. Badabing Badaboom you've done it
  9. Add one gold star to gold star collection
